Fix A Trip Puerto Rico — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, and phone number when you make a booking through our website at fixatrippuertorico.vercel.app/booking.

2. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent checkbox on our booking form, you explicitly agree to receive text messages from Fix A Trip PR at the number provided.

SMS messages we send include:

  • Booking confirmations
  • Activity reminders (sent the day before your experience)
  • Real-time trip updates (weather changes, schedule adjustments)
  • Post-experience feedback requests

Message frequency varies by booking. Message & data rates may apply.

SMS consent is obtained through a clearly visible, unchecked checkbox on the booking form at fixatrippuertorico.vercel.app/booking. The form cannot be submitted without acknowledging this section.

3. How to Opt Out (STOP / HELP)

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Reply HELP to any message for assistance.

You can also contact us at info@fixatrippr.com or call (787) 370-0218 to opt out.

4. We Do Not Share Your Data

Your phone number and personal information are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is used solely to deliver your booked experience and related communications.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.
